Mister Wednesday

Bilquis

Ladies and gentlemen, please welcome to the ring The Queen of Sheba herself: the legendary, lovable, adorable, biblical Bilquis!

The godess Bilquis has guile, agility, and physical strength to burn. But she likes to take her time and savour every movement, so speed is not among her most worshippable characteristics.

Mister Nancy

Originally hailing from West Africa and now fighting out of West African, African American, and Caribbean folklore, ladies and gentleman, please welcome to the ring, the trickster, the spider, Mis-ter Anansi!

Anansi is cunning, quick, and agile, but may lack in brute strength.

Czernobog

Fighting out of 12th century Christian chronicles and 13th century Icelandic legend, ladies and gentlemen, I present to you the Beast from the East, the ancient slavic god himself - Chernobog!

Chernobog's physical strength is of the charts, but he's not the sharpest tool in the slaughterhouse
