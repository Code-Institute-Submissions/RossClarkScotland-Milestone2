American Gods by Neil Gaiman is a supernatural, historical, sort-of-but-not-really horror, urban fantasy road trip, instant cult classic of a novel. But if you're a Technical Boy, Technical Girl or Technical Anywhere In Between who's interacting with this collection of ones and zeros, then you probably already knew that. Nevertheless, the (spoiler-free) story follows Shadow, a recently released former convict, and the mysterious Wednesday as they traverse The United States to rally support among the gods of the old world for a coming war with the gods of the new. Many readers, and now viewers since the novel has been adapted into a hit TV show, dream of following in Shadow’s tire tracks by recreating his road trip. This site is here to help.
