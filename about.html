About

American Gods by Neil Gaiman is a supernatural, historical, sort-of-but-not-really horror, urban fantasy road trip, instant cult classic of a novel. The (spoiler-free) story follows Shadow, a recently released former convict, and the mysterious Wednesday as they traverse The United States to rally support among the gods of the old world for a coming war with the gods of the new. Many readers, and now viewers since the novel has been adapted into a hit TV show, dream of following in Shadow’s tire tracks by recreating his road trip. This site is here to help. File under ‘nerdy geography’.

NERDY NOTE: The geography on this site does not refer to that in the TV show but to that of the novel (the tenth anniversary edition) and the 2020 graphic novels, with supporting info sourced from Fandom.com. Furthermore, this site follow’s Shadow’s road trip, i.e. not he and Wednesday’s airborne jaunts to Las Vegas and the like, and not the travels of the supporting cast of characters.
