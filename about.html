American Gods by Neil Gaiman is a supernatural, historical, sort-of-but-not-really horror, urban fantasy road trip, instant cult classic of a novel. It takes place on American back roads less travelled by and has inspired numerous readers to follow Shadow's, the main character's, tire tracks to discover an America that doesn't often appear on the silver screen. This site is here for you if you want to make that trip, or if you just want to find out more about the locations in the novel (Nerd note: we focus on the novel here, but there is of course a lot of overlap with the TV show).
