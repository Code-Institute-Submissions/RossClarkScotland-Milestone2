American Gods by Neil Gaiman is a supernatural, historical, sort-of-but-not-really horror, urban fantasy road trip, instant cult classic of a novel. It takes place on American back roads less travelled by and has inspired numerous readers to follow Shadow's, the main character's, tire tracks to discover an America that doesn't often appear on the silver screen.
